Welcome to ASL Salon

A Refreshing Escape in Louisville

Located at 1927 South Hurstbourne Parkway, surrounded by diverse restaurants and Malibu Jacks, our salon is designed to be your sanctuary.

With spacious interiors, we are equipped to comfortably serve multiple clients simultaneously. Our skilled team stays at the top of the latest trends and techniques in the beauty industry to ensure you leave looking and feeling fabulous.
