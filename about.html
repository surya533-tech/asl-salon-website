Welcome to ASL Salon & Spa

A Refreshing Escape in Louisville

Located at 1927 South Hurstbourne Parkway, KY 40220 — nestled in a vibrant area surrounded by diverse international restaurants and the popular play center, Malibu Jacks — our salon is designed to be a refreshing escape.

With spacious interiors, we're equipped to comfortably serve multiple clients simultaneously, complete with amenities like a clean bathroom and ample parking for your convenience.

Our skilled and friendly team of professional hair stylists is here to ensure you leave looking and feeling fabulous. Whether it's a fresh haircut, styling, or specialized skin care, our priority is to cater to your unique needs, making your visit a truly enjoyable experience.

Step into our salon for a moment of self-care, and let us help you look your best. Your satisfaction is our Goal!

  • English, Nepali & Hindi spoken
  • Ample free parking
